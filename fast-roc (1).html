Who We Are

Because this relationship MATTERS.

"We envision a thriving, resilient future where African American and BIPOC families see themselves as powerful agents of transformation — grounded in culture, connected in purpose, and united in love."

Fathers & Sons Together (FAST) is a South Seattle nonprofit rooted in Rainier Beach, dedicated to Empowering, Developing, Sustaining, and Supporting (EDSS) family relationships. Through culturally rooted programs, FAST builds leadership, wellness, and generational resilience in African American and BIPOC households.

The FAST ROC connects community members to vetted, local services across employment, housing, mental health, legal aid, education, and more. Our flagship youth program, Next Generation Level Up (NGLU), serves young people ages 14–24 through career readiness, mentorship, and pathway-to-success coaching.
